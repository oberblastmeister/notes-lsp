**Warmup**: $\frac{d}{dx}(x^3 - 1)^2$

Let $$\begin{align*}
  f(x) &= x^2 \\
  g(x) &= x^3 - 1 \\
  (x^3 - 1)^2 &= (f \circ g)(x)
\end{align*}$$

**Definition**: If $f$ and $g$ are both differentiable, and
$$h(x) = (f \circ g)(x)$$
So
$$h'(x) = (f' \circ g)(x) * g'(x)$$

**Example 1**:

Three functions $f$ $g$ $h$.
Derivative is $$(f' \circ g \circ h)(x) * (g' \circ h)(x) * h'(x)$$
